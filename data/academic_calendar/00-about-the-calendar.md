# FGCU Academic Calendar - Overview


Add/drop and withdrawal deadlines end at 5:00 pm on the date listed. Before the add/drop deadline you drop a class in Gulfline for a full refund; after that you must submit a withdrawal form and the course shows a W. Unless a student names a specific session (Session A, B, I, II, or Summer A/B/C), use the full-term dates for the semester.
