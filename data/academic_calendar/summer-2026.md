# Summer 2026 (Full Term)


These are the main dates for the standard Summer 2026 semester. The accelerated 7-week and 8-week sessions (Session A, B, I, II, and Summer A/B) have different dates - use those only if the student specifically asks about a session.

- Last day to add or drop a class (100% refund), ends 5:00 pm: Friday, May 15, 2026
- Last day to withdraw without academic penalty, ends 5:00 pm: Monday, June 22, 2026
- Last day of classes: Saturday, July 18, 2026
