# Spring 2027 (Full Term)


These are the main dates for the standard Spring 2027 semester. The accelerated 7-week and 8-week sessions (Session A, B, I, II, and Summer A/B) have different dates - use those only if the student specifically asks about a session.

- Classes begin: Monday, January 11, 2027
- Last day to add or drop a class (100% refund), ends 5:00 pm: Friday, January 15, 2027
- Last day to withdraw without academic penalty, ends 5:00 pm: Friday, March 19, 2027
- Last day of classes: Saturday, April 24, 2027
- Finals end: Friday, April 30, 2027
- Commencement: Saturday, May 1, 2027
