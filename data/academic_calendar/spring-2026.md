# Spring 2026 (Full Term)


These are the main dates for the standard Spring 2026 semester. The accelerated 7-week and 8-week sessions (Session A, B, I, II, and Summer A/B) have different dates - use those only if the student specifically asks about a session.

- Classes begin: Wednesday, January 7, 2026
- Last day to add or drop a class (100% refund), ends 5:00 pm: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- Last day to withdraw without academic penalty, ends 5:00 pm: Tuesday, March 24, 2026
- Last day of classes: Monday, April 27, 2026
- Finals end: Saturday, May 2, 2026
- Commencement: Saturday, 02 May 2026 - Sunday, 03 May 2026
