# Fall 2026 (Full Term)


These are the main dates for the standard Fall 2026 semester. The accelerated 7-week and 8-week sessions (Session A, B, I, II, and Summer A/B) have different dates - use those only if the student specifically asks about a session.

- Classes begin: Monday, August 17, 2026
- Last day to add or drop a class (100% refund), ends 5:00 pm: Friday, August 21, 2026
- Last day to withdraw without academic penalty, ends 5:00 pm: Friday, October 23, 2026
- Last day of classes: Tuesday, November 24, 2026
- Finals end: Friday, December 4, 2026
- Commencement: Saturday, 05 December 2026 - Sunday, 06 December 2026
