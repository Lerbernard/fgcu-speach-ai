Note: Topic: :Research Methods for Computer Science This course equips students to design, conduct, and communicate rigorous, reproducible CS research. Students will practice literature mapping, research questions, hypothesis formulation, study design, basic statistics/ethics, and open-science workflows. By the end, students produce a reproducible artifact (code/data), a conference-style paper, and a polished short talk in their chosen domain
