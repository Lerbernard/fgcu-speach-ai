Note: This course explores the key concepts governing groundwater movement and the transport of dissolved substances within aquifer systems, with an emphasis on how subsurface flow interacts with environmental processes. Students will develop a strong conceptual understanding of aquifer behavior and contaminant dynamics, preparing them for advanced coursework and professional roles in water resources and environmental engineering
