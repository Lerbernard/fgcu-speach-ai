# EGS 1006L — Course Description

Subject: EGS | Course: EGS 1006L

EGS 1006L
 Intro to the Engineering Prof. - 1 credits(s)
 The course provides an overview of the important aspects of the engineering disciplines offered at FGCU, including professional components, career opportunities, the role of the engineering approach to problem-solving, and the engineering design process. The course is built around active learning concepts as well as team-based learning. ~ Previously
