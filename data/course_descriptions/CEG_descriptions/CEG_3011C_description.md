# CEG 3011C — Course Description

Subject: CEG | Course: CEG 3011C

CEG 3011C
 Geotechnical Engineering I - 3 credits(s)
 Physical properties and classification of soils; flow of water through soil, distribution of stress within soil and consolidation; design consideration for compaction, seepage, and settlement analysis; laboratory. Technical written communication.
 Prerequisite(s)
