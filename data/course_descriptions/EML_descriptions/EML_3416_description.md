# EML 3416 — Course Description

Subject: EML | Course: EML 3416

EML 3416
 Science & Tech of Solar Energy - 3 credits(s)
 Introduces the concept of solar energy and its utilization on Earth for heat, electricity and hot water. Covers fundamental concepts, applications and know-how with special attention to the design, manufacturing and installation of solar modules.
 Prerequisite(s)
