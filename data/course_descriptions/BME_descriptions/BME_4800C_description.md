# BME 4800C — Course Description

Subject: BME | Course: BME 4800C

BME 4800C
 Bioengineering Product Design - 3 credits(s)
 This course emphasizes hands-on, applied learning, encouraging students to integrate sustainability knowledge into team-based engineering projects. THE ENGINEERING DESIGN PROCESS is introduced and APPLIED TO BIOENGINEERED PRODUCTS. The course FOCUSES ON TECHNICAL, environmental, REGULATORY, LEGAL, ETHICAL, ECONOMIC, AND SOCIAL ASPECTS OF MEDICAL DEVICES, BIOLOGIC AND COMBINATION PRODUCT DESIGN, DEVELOPMENT, AND COMMERCIALIZATION. This course INCLUDES benchmarking of EXISTING PRODUCTS, design applied to novel products, AND PROVIDES AN INTRODUCTION TO MODERN ENGINEERING SOFTWARE TOOLS FOR BIOMEDICAL PRODUCT DESIGN.
 Prerequisite(s)
