# CIS 2941 — Course Description

Subject: CIS | Course: CIS 2941

CIS 2941
 Intro Internship In Comp Sci - 0 credits(s)
 A 0-credit experiential learning course that provides students with the opportunity to gain practical exposure in the computing fields. Students engage in supervised work or professional experience, paid or unpaid, that supports their development in the computing disciplines. Participation requires active involvement in industry-related activities for the duration of the course.
 Prerequisite(s)
