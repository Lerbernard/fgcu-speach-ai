# EES 4102C — Course Description

Subject: EES | Course: EES 4102C

EES 4102C
 Wastewater Microbiology - 3 credits(s)
 General concepts in microbiology with major emphasis on the role of microorganisms in polluted environments. Focuses on the biota (microorganisms, algae, zooplankton, fish, and plants) found in natural (lakes and wetlands) and engineered systems, ecological engineering approach to management of surface waters and ecological modeling.
 Prerequisite(s)
