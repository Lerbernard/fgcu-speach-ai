# CWR 6637 — Course Description

Subject: CWR | Course: CWR 6637

CWR 6637
 Storm Water Mgmt and Modeling - 3 credits(s)
 Principles governing the transport and fate of contaminants in surface water systems. Water quality standards, wastewater inputs, water quality modeling for water-borne disease, dissolved oxygen, and toxic chemicals. Engineering controls to meet water quality objectives and case studies are presented.
