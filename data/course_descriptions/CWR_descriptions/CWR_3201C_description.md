# CWR 3201C — Course Description

Subject: CWR | Course: CWR 3201C

CWR 3201C
 Engineering Fluid Mechanics - 3 credits(s)
 Fundamentals of fluid mechanics; properties of fluids, hydrostatics; Bernoulli principle; conservation of mass, momentum and energy in fluid flow; laminar and turbulent pipe flow; similitude and dimensional analysis; and flow measurements; laboratory.
 Prerequisite(s)
