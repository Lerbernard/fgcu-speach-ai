# CAI 5537 — Course Description

Subject: CAI | Course: CAI 5537

CAI 5537
 Int Syst & Aug Mixed Reality - 3 credits(s)
 Graduate exploration of intelligent systems for augmented and mixed reality. Topics include multimodal perception, vision-language models, 3D mapping, spatial interaction, on-device inference, ethics, and evaluation. Students build prototypes, conduct IRB-aware studies, deliver publishable reports, and demos.
