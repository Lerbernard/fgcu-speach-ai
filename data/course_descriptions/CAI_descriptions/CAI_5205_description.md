Subject: CAI | Course: CAI 5205

CAI 5205
 Adv Neural Net & Deep Learning - 3 credits(s)
 This course focuses on advanced concepts and applications of artificial neural networks and deep learning, exploring the cutting-edge developments in the field. Participants will gain an in-depth understanding of the latest architectures, optimization techniques, and emerging trends in neural network research and applications.
