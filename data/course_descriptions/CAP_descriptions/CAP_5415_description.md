# CAP 5415 — Course Description

Subject: CAP | Course: CAP 5415

CAP 5415
 Computer Vision - 3 credits(s)
 A comprehensive introduction to the analysis of images and video, focusing on the recognition, reconstruction, and modeling of objects in the three-dimensional world. Includes image formation principles, camera imaging geometry, feature detection and matching, stereo vision, motion estimation, tracking, image classification, and scene understanding.
