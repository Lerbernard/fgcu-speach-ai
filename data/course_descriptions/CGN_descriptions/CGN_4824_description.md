# CGN 4824 — Course Description

Subject: CGN | Course: CGN 4824

CGN 4824
 Land Development - 3 credits(s)
 This course provides an in-depth exploration of the land development process, highlighting the crucial role of civil engineers in developing site plans, paving, grading, and drainage plans, and utility plans. Students will also gain insight into the design and calculation of stormwater management systems and utility infrastructure. The course is designed to equip students with the practical skills and knowledge necessary to effectively contribute to land development projects, ensuring compliance with environmental, legal, and community standards.
