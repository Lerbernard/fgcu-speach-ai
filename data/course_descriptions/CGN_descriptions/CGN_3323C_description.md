# CGN 3323C — Course Description

Subject: CGN | Course: CGN 3323C

CGN 3323C
 Surveying and Geomatics - 3 credits(s)
 Introduction to elementary surveying practices such as autolevel, total station, global positioning system (GPS) as well as covering highlights of construction materials, tools, and techniques. In addition geomatics, the study of spatially related data, will be introduced. Instruction will cover the integrated approach of measurement, analysis, and management of the descriptions and locations of Earth-based data. This course is inquiry based and fully integrated with field experience that emphasizes active learning strategies.
 Prerequisite(s)
