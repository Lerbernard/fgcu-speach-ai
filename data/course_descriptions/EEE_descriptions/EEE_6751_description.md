# EEE 6751 — Course Description

Subject: EEE | Course: EEE 6751

EEE 6751
 Adv Image & Video Forensics - 3 credits(s)
 This course provides students the opportunity to explore advanced techniques for forensic analysis, authentication, and tamper detection in multimedia content, particularly in image and video. Includes digital image and video forensics principles, forensic analysis of compression artifacts, camera identification, forgery detection, and steganography.
