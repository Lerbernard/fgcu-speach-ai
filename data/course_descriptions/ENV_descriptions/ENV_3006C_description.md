# ENV 3006C — Course Description

Subject: ENV | Course: ENV 3006C

ENV 3006C
 Fundamentals of Environ Engrg - 3 credits(s)
 Environmental engineering dimensions and units. Material and energy balances, kinetics, stoichiometry, and reactors. Ecosystems. Water Quality, Water Supply, Water Treatment. Wastewater Collection, Wastewater Treatment, Wastewater Re-use. Solid Wastes, Hazardous Wastes. Air Quality, Atmospheric Pollution Control, Climate Change. Risk. Laboratory Safety.
