# ENV 6027 — Course Description

Subject: ENV | Course: ENV 6027

ENV 6027
 Bioremediation - 3 credits(s)
 The basic principles of chemical and biological degradation of toxic chemicals, and familiarize the students with the application of the remedial technologies in natural environments. Topics covered will include: 1) occurrence and ecological significance of toxic organic chemicals, 2) chemistry of contaminants, kinetics and mechanisms of degradation (chemical and biological), and 3) current technologies of bioremediation of contaminated soils and water.
