# ENV 4509C — Course Description

Subject: ENV | Course: ENV 4509C

ENV 4509C
 Wastewater Engineering - 3 credits(s)
 The theory and the design of processes normally used in the practice of environmental engineering for the treatment of wastes. Biological Processes important to the cycling of carbon, nitrogen, phosphorus and energy in ecosystems; the impact of biological systems on pollutant fate and transport.
 Prerequisite(s)
