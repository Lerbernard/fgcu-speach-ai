# ENV 4891 — Course Description

Subject: ENV | Course: ENV 4891

ENV 4891
 Envir Engr Senior Design - 3 credits(s)
 The design of operations, processes and systems used in environmental engineering for protection and remediation of water, air and soil ecosystems. Consideration of ethical , professional licensing, economic and sustainability issues. Rational comparison of alternatives. Technical written communication.
 Prerequisite(s)
