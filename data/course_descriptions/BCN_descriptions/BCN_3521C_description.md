# BCN 3521C — Course Description

Subject: BCN | Course: BCN 3521C

BCN 3521C
 Electrical Systems - 3 credits(s)
 Principles and practices of electrical and lighting systems used in residential, commercial and industrial applications. Emphasis is placed on coordination of the National Electrical Code (NEC), design, and installation of various systems in the building process.
