# BCN 2949 — Course Description

Subject: BCN | Course: BCN 2949

BCN 2949
 Intro Internship in Const - 0 credits(s)
 A 0-credit experiential learning course that provides students with the opportunity to gain practical exposure to the construction industry. Students engage in supervised work or professional experience, paid or unpaid, that supports their development and connects to the Construction Management discipline. Participation requires active involvement in an industry-related role for the duration of the course.
 Prerequisite(s)
