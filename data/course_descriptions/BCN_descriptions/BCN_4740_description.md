# BCN 4740 — Course Description

Subject: BCN | Course: BCN 4740

BCN 4740
 Construction Law & Contracts - 3 credits(s)
 Basic construction contracts, specifications, and law. Discussion of the application of contract principles to construction contracts, together with a detail summary of specifications and other documents which form an essential part of construction contracts. Legal issues arising from design and construction services, focusing on risk management and liability awareness.
 Prerequisite(s)
