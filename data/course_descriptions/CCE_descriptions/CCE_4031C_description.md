# CCE 4031C — Course Description

Subject: CCE | Course: CCE 4031C

CCE 4031C
 Project Planning & Regulations - 3 credits(s)
 Introduction to techniques for planning activities, operations, finance, budget, workforce, quality, safety, and other issues related to civil and environmental engineering planning. This includes the process of locating, discovering, and studying local, state, and federal regulations/codes. The curriculum is integrated with laboratory exercises that emphasize blueprint reading, quantity takeoff and learning software packages used in cost estimating and project scheduling and controls.
 Prerequisite(s)
