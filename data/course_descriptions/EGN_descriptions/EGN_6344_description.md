# EGN 6344 — Course Description

Subject: EGN | Course: EGN 6344

EGN 6344
 Environmental Thermodynamics - 3 credits(s)
 First and second laws of thermodynamics with applications in Biosystems, engineering and chemical reactions. Enthalpy, Gibbs Free Energy and Entropy of Chemical reactions and mixing. Heat and maas transfer. Refrigeration cycles, entropy, thermodynamic aspects of fluid flow and psychometrics.
