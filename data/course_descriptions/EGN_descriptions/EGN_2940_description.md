# EGN 2940 — Course Description

Subject: EGN | Course: EGN 2940

EGN 2940
 Intro Engineering Internship - 0 credits(s)
 A 0-credit experiential learning course that provides students with the opportunity to gain practical exposure in bioengineering, civil engineering, or environmental engineering settings. Students engage in supervised work or professional experience, paid or unpaid, that supports their professional development and connects directly to their respective engineering discipline. Participation requires active involvement in an industry-related role for the duration of the course.
 Prerequisite(s)
