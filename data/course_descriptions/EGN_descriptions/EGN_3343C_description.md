# EGN 3343C — Course Description

Subject: EGN | Course: EGN 3343C

EGN 3343C
 Thermodynamics - 3 credits(s)
 Fundamental concepts of basic thermodynamics including first and second law topics, equations of state and general thermodynamic relationships. Work, heat and energy transformations. Relation of properties. Laws, concepts and modes of analysis common to all applications of thermodynamics in engineering.
