# EGN 4940 — Course Description

Subject: EGN | Course: EGN 4940

EGN 4940
 Engineering Internship - 1 TO 3 credits(s)
 An experiential learning exercise that allows for practical application of knowledge acquired in the classroom. Applicable to unpaid internships held within the current academic year or subsequent summer. This work is usually completed off campus and the student will have a qualified supervisor at the site as well as a faculty mentor who will jointly evaluate the student's progress.
 Attribute(s)
 WBLI - Work based learning indicator
