# EGN 4908 — Course Description

Subject: EGN | Course: EGN 4908

EGN 4908
 Engineering Independent Study - 1 TO 4 credits(s)
 Independent study for students under the direction of a faculty mentor. The topic will be determined between the student, the faculty mentor, and the department chair. Instructor permission required. This course may not cover engineering topics already covered in the curriculum.
