# U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering

At FGCU's U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering (WCE), we help students turn big ideas into real solutions. Whether you dream of managing water systems, building accessible software, improving roads for our growing population, or designing resilient buildings that can withstand hurricanes, our programs prepare you to make a difference.

As a WCE student, you'll learn by doing. Our students get hands-on experience, work closely with expert professors, and build projects that solve real problems right here in our own community.

## Departments
Department of Bioengineering, Civil Engineering, and Environmental Engineering — prepares students to address current issues using engineering tools to identify problems and work toward sustainable solutions.

Department of Computing and Software Engineering — prepares students for a variety of careers in computer and information technology occupations in cutting-edge areas.

Stock Development Department of Construction Management — the first named department at FGCU, prepares students for careers in the construction management field.
