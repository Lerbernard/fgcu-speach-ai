# FGCU Student Government

FGCU Student Government is committed to providing an intellectual, social, and cultural university environment that maximizes student potential, promotes individuality, and enhances student success. Its values are Engagement, Approachability, and Awareness.

Student Government has three branches: the Legislative Branch, the Executive Branch, and the Judicial Branch.

## Public Meetings
All students, staff, faculty, and community members are invited to attend public meetings:
- Executive Cabinet — Wednesdays at 3:00 pm — Cohen 249
- Student Body Senate — Tuesdays at 6:30 pm — Cohen Student Union 247
- Supreme Court — Mondays biweekly at 10:00 am — Cohen 245
