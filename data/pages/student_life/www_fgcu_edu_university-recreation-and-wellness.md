# University Recreation & Wellness

University Recreation & Wellness empowers the FGCU community to actively pursue holistic wellness.

The University Recreation & Wellness Center is open to the FGCU community; facility hours and membership information are available on the website. The Rec Store is the one-stop shop for registrations, including outdoor trips and Intramural Sports. FGCU's Waterfront at Lake Como is the designated swimming and sunbathing area for students and is not intended for public use.

## Programs
- Prevention & Wellness: Provides students with health education and resources to improve overall well-being.
- Fitness: Free group fitness classes offered daily for all skill levels; personal training available to students, faculty and staff.
- Intramural Sports: Organized competitive or recreational sports for all skill levels, open to students, faculty and staff.
- RecVentures: Learn to sail at the Waterfront, go on day trips, or check out a bike through the Pedal Power Bike Share program.
- Sport Clubs: Formed by students with a common interest in an activity, with over 30 clubs to choose from, competing against other universities.

## Facilities
Aquatics Center, University Recreation & Wellness Center, Recreation Field 1, Recreation Field 2, Recreation Outdoor Complex (ROC), SoVi Pool, Waterfront, and Prevention & Wellness.
