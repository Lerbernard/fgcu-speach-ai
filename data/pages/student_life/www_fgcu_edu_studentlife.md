# Student Life at FGCU

With 15,000 students, FGCU is a lively, diverse and welcoming community with a reputation as one of the nation's safest campuses. Getting involved is key to a successful and memorable college experience.

## By the Numbers
- 1/3 of students live on campus
- 250+ clubs & organizations
- 14 places to eat on campus

FGCU's Waterfront at Lake Como is the designated swimming and sunbathing area for students; it is not intended for public use.

## Live Where You Learn
State-of-the-art residence halls make life easier while enriching your social world, just minutes from classes, the gym and dining options. Related areas include Housing, Getting Around, University Recreation & Wellness, Fraternity/Sorority Life, and Service Learning.

## Get More Out of College
Hundreds of organizations and sports clubs can enhance your Eagle experience, from Quidditch and paintball to yoga and the Food Forest.

## Health and Safety
FGCU provides physical and emotional well-being services to ensure student success, including counseling, student health, and prevention and wellness programs.
