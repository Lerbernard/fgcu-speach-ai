# Retention & Graduation Services

Retention & Graduation Services encompasses a range of support mechanisms and resources designed to help students persist in their academic studies, overcome challenges, and ultimately graduate from their programs. The office aims to improve student success rates and enhance graduation outcomes by connecting students to the right campus and community resources at the right time.

## Transition Programs
Transition Programs at FGCU help students navigate the academic, social, and personal transitions that come with entering college, ensuring they are well-prepared for the opportunities and responsibilities ahead. This includes Eagle View Orientation and Student Programs.
