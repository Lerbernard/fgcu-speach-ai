# Dean of Students Office

The Dean of Students Office is home to Student Care Services, Student Conduct, and Basic Needs Initiatives. Staff provide students with support, resources, and accountability during their time at FGCU.

Areas and services include Student Care Services, Student Conduct, Basic Needs Initiatives, and an A–Z Services directory.

## Concerns or Complaints
For academic or non-academic student complaints, visit the Student Complaints page to familiarize yourself with the relevant policies and procedures.
