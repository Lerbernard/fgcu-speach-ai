# Basic Needs Initiatives (BNI)

Basic Needs Initiatives (BNI) offers personalized academic and personal support to students with lived experience in foster care, homelessness, or ward of the state status, helping them thrive at FGCU and beyond. The mission is to support students from the moment they arrive at FGCU through graduation and beyond, connecting them to resources so no Eagle has to choose between life's challenges and their education.

## Services

**Academic & Career Support**
- Academic and life skills workshops
- One-on-one mentoring and guidance
- Career and graduate school preparation

**Financial & Resource Navigation**
- Financial literacy and FAFSA guidance
- DCF/Homeless waiver assistance
- Referrals to campus and community resources

**Student Advocacy**
- Ongoing case management and student support
- Collaboration with campus partners to reduce barriers to success

## Make a Referral
If a student is living without stable housing — on a friend's couch or in a car — BNI can assist with finding temporary housing, financial support, and academic resources. Students from foster care can be connected with campus and community resources. Resources include a Housing Resource Guide, the DCF/Homeless Waiver Application, and Student Resources.
