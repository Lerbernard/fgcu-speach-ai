# Student Organizations & Involvement

Participating in an organization helps prepare you to live in an engaged society. The Office of Student Involvement (OSI) supports student organizations and involvement.

## Resources and Programs
- OSI Ambassadors: Student leaders who help promote the benefits and ways of getting involved.
- Purchasing Resources: For Registered Student Organizations.
- Registered Student Organization Advisors: A full-time FGCU faculty or staff member may advise an organization (including outside their field of study), may advise more than one organization, can store an RSO's equipment and supplies in their campus office, must complete the Advisor Acknowledgement Form, and is encouraged to attend RSO and Advisor Trainings.
- Eagle Link Resources: Help with maximizing Eagle Link for events and activities, and support for an RSO's Eagle Link page.
- Advisor Toolkit: Includes Eagle Link Training Videos, Finance Code, and Senate Bill Forms (completed by RSO officers only).

## RSO Registration
Re-registration for 2025–2026 has closed and will reopen in March 2026. Registration for a new RSO will also open in March 2026. To keep an RSO active for Fall 2026–Spring 2027, the re-registration process must be completed in March 2026. Contact OSI by email for assistance.
