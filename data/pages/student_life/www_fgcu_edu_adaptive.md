# FGCU Adaptive Services

Adaptive Services enhances access for students, faculty, staff and guests with disabilities by providing effective, reasonable accommodations, educating the campus community, and promoting equal access and opportunity.

## How to Request Accommodations

**Prospective Students & Family Members**
Prospective students or family members with questions can call 239-590-7956, email pus at adaptive@fgcu.edu or find information under the Prospective Student tab.

**First Time Registration for Incoming/Current Students**
Incoming students for the Summer/Fall 2026 semester, or current students needing accommodations for the first time, should contact Adaptive Services to learn about first-time accommodation requests.

**Students Currently Registered with Adaptive Services**
Students currently registered should refer to the Adaptive Services Canvas page for all information regarding receiving accommodations and services in person and virtually.

**Faculty & Staff**
Employees of FGCU can request reasonable accommodations and services related to a disability by contacting Adaptive Services and setting up an appointment. Information on the process and required forms is in the Faculty/Staff Resource Guide. Supervisors are required to refer any employee requesting assistance to Adaptive Services. (Canvas access requires Google Chrome.)

FGCU, under the guidelines of federal legislation, provides access to activities, programs and services to students, faculty, staff and guests with disabilities. Not registered students use the Registration portal; already registered students use the Clockwork Portal.
