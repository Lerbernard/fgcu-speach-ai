# TRIO and Outreach Programs

TRIO and Outreach Programs provide opportunities for students to advance their academic and personal growth, gain financial responsibility, explore careers, and understand college requirements. FGCU brings together 5 grant-based programs that help students prepare to succeed in college and stay on track to finishing a degree.

## Student Support Services
An educational opportunity designed to motivate and support college students.

## Outreach Programs
Motivating and preparing precollege students for postsecondary education.
