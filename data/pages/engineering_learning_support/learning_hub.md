# The Learning Hub

The Learning Hub provides academic support and peer mentoring for U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering students in their engineering, computing, or construction classes.

Location: Holmes 203. Open Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 5 PM. Staffed by upper-level students called fellows who assist with engineering, computing, and construction management courses. Fellows are successful students, not professors or experts, and are not responsible for your final grade. Contact: 239-745-4310 or wceinfo@fgcu.edu.
