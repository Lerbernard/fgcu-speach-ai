# Office of Internships & Cooperative Programs (ICP)

The Office of Internships & Cooperative Programs (ICP) is your campus connection to information and resources on internships and other work-based learning experiences, serving students, employers, faculty, and staff.

Resources are available for Students, Employers, and Faculty & Staff.

## Eagle Career Network
FGCU's official career management system. The Eagle Career Network offers internships & cooperative programs, full- and part-time opportunities, upcoming career fairs and events, exclusive workshops, and networking opportunities. New opportunities show up every day, and it is available 24/7 via web browser or mobile app.
