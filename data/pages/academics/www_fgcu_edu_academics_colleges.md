# Colleges & Schools at FGCU

Each college and school delivers challenging academics, experiential learning, and comprehensive support — common denominators for success here and after graduation.

## Colleges
- College of Arts & Sciences: Interdisciplinary collaboration on research and scholarship strengthens individual fields of study in the arts, humanities, natural and social sciences.
- Lutgert College of Business: Learn to think critically and move confidently from the classroom to the boardroom. Career paths include accounting, finance, management and marketing. Internships play a critical role.
- College of Education: Programs for teachers, educational administrators and other professional school personnel explore theory, research and emerging technologies in teaching, learning and curriculum development.
- U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering: Degree programs in bioengineering, civil engineering, environmental engineering, software engineering, computer science and construction management prepare graduates for well-paid, in-demand careers.
- Marieb College of Health & Human Services: Programs are grounded in professional skills, critical thinking and clinical experience. Marieb graduates are well prepared to excel on licensing exams and succeed in the ever-changing healthcare field.
- Honors College: Honors curricula and related activities are designed to offer high-achieving students from all majors enhanced opportunities and resources so that they grow and develop intellectually, culturally and socially.

## Schools
- Bower School of Music & the Arts: Within the College of Arts & Sciences, offers education in music performance and theory, music therapy, art and theatre. The Arts Complex and Music Building form the cultural heart of campus.
- Daveler & Kauanui School of Entrepreneurship: Bachelor's and master's degrees prepare you to join the next generation of innovators, visionaries and industry-shaping leaders.
- School of Nursing: Student-centered nursing education and strong clinical partnerships in Southwest Florida. Most programs deliver 100% graduate employment rates and excellent first-time certification pass rates.
- School of Resort & Hospitality Management: With 100% placement in paid internships, degree programs prepare you to take your place among the leaders in event, hotel and club management.
- The Water School: Located amid Florida's complex freshwater and saltwater systems, FGCU is uniquely positioned to explore water-based issues, studying waterway health and researching solutions benefiting the environment, public health and economy.
