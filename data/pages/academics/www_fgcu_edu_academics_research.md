# Office of Research and Sponsored Programs (ORSP)

The Office of Research and Sponsored Programs (ORSP) supports and manages sponsored projects that advance the institution's scholarship and research profile.

Areas include: Institutional Review Board (IRB), Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC), PreAward, PostAward, Grant Funding Sources, Emergent Technologies Institute (ETI), Research Compliance, and ORSP Events.

## Fast Facts
- $404 Million in research funding since opening in 1997
- $43.8 Million in research funding for fiscal year 2024–2025

## Centers & Institutes
- Lucas Institute for Real Estate Development & Finance
- Shady Rest Institute on Positive Aging
- Whitaker Institute for STEM Education

Establishing and reviewing centers and institutes at FGCU is governed by Policy 2.001.
