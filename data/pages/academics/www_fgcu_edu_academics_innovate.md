# Innovative Education & Partnerships

FGCU offers a dynamic range of educational opportunities, including certificates, degrees and specialized programs like micro-credentials and digital badges. Designed for the evolving needs of today's learners and the workforce of Southwest Florida, these programs provide flexible, high-quality options.

Offerings include:
- Degree Completion: Unique programs with flexible options to start or finish your bachelor's degree.
- Micro-credentials & Digital Badges: Designed for current and future FGCU students and SWFL community members.
- Continuing Education & Professional Development: Non-credit programs designed for upskilling, reskilling and professional development.
- Graduate Studies: A range of master's, doctoral, and certificate programs with flexible options for working professionals.
