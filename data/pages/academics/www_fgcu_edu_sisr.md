# Office of Scholarly Innovation & Student Research (SISR)

The Office of Scholarly Innovation & Student Research (SISR) promotes student research.

## Opportunities
- WiSER Student Research Internship: Promotes student engagement in research.
- Student Research Funds: Support graduate and undergraduate research and creative work through funding for conference travel or research supplies.
- Presentation Opportunities: SISR promotes student opportunities to present their research, creative, and scholarly work at on- and off-campus conferences.
- Publication Opportunities: SISR promotes opportunities for students to publish their scholarly and creative works in on- and off-campus journals.
- Faculty Research Database (ScholarsCommons): FGCU's portal for showcasing scholarship, expertise, and activities, allowing the public a window into the community and its work.
- Roadmap to Success: Students can customize their roadmap of opportunities to specialize their path toward career-readiness while tracking successes and achievements.

## About SISR
Fostering scholarship in the library, in the lab, in the studio, and in the community.

## Fast Facts (AY 2024–25)
- SISR provided 89 research assistantships.
- 120 students were awarded research funds from SISR to conduct research or present at an off-campus conference.
