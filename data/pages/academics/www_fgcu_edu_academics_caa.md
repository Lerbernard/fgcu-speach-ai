# Center for Academic Achievement (CAA)

The CAA offers free resources to help all FGCU students maximize their academic potential, including free academic coaching, tutoring, supplemental instruction, and writing consultations.

Services include Supplemental Instruction, the Writing Lab, Tutoring, and Academic Coaching.

Brainfuse is a free online tutoring platform for FGCU students, offering support that matches busy schedules and helps distance learners who can't make it to in-person tutoring.

## Request the CAA
The CAA provides the opportunity for faculty, staff, organizations, and departments to request the CAA during classes and events. Topics include Academic Standing, Goal Setting (Dream Big), Time Management Study Hour Formula, SQ3R Reading Comprehension, Study Group Management Kit, Anti-Cramming Test Prep, Final Exams Prep Kit, and Goal Setting Action Plan.
