# Construction Management Department Overview

Construction Managers coordinate activities and work with a wide variety of professionals including architects, engineers, financial experts, suppliers, and public stakeholders.

The Bachelor of Science in Construction Management (B.S.C.M.) emphasizes public and private, commercial and residential construction with a focus on sustainable construction methods. The program employs a team-based interdisciplinary learning philosophy that provides students with critical thinking skills and the technologies needed to solve problems and deliver successful construction projects.

The department is officially named the **Stock Development Department of Construction Management** following a $2.5 million gift from Stock Development.

## Programs Offered
- Construction Management (B.S.C.M.)
- Construction Management Certificate
- Construction Concentration (M.S.)

## Construction Industry Advisory Board (CIAB)
The CIAB includes industry members who review and advise on the curriculum to ensure students are prepared for real-world construction management careers.
