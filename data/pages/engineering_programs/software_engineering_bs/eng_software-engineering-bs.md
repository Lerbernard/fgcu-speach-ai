# Software Engineering B.S. — Program Overview

## What Do Software Engineers Do?
Software engineers use a systematic approach to the software life cycle including development, production, operation, and maintenance of large-scale software projects.

## What is the Software Engineering Program at FGCU Like?
- Learn to code in Python and C++ with a combination of computer science and software engineering courses
- Small class sizes and accessible faculty
- Undergraduate research in AI, cybersecurity, and data science
- Student organizations like the Computer Science and Software Engineering Club (CSSEC)
- Specialized courses including human computer interaction, robotics, natural language processing, digital systems, neural networks, data mining, machine learning, and assembly language

## Where Do Software Engineers Work?
- Application and System Development
- Embedded Systems
- Computer Games
- Operating and Network Systems
- Graduate studies including M.S. in Computer Science

74% of FGCU software engineering graduates stay in Florida.
Average alumni salary: $76,144.

## Accreditation
The B.S. Software Engineering program at FGCU is accredited by the Engineering Accreditation Commission of ABET.

## Program Educational Objectives
Graduates are expected to within a few years of graduation:
- Leverage software engineering principles to contribute meaningfully in professional roles or graduate studies
- Practice life-long learning, adapting to the rapidly changing technological world

## Student Outcomes
By graduation, students will have:
- Ability to identify, formulate, and solve complex engineering problems
- Ability to apply engineering design with consideration of public health, safety, and welfare
- Ability to communicate effectively with a range of audiences
- Ability to recognize ethical and professional responsibilities
- Ability to function effectively on a team
- Ability to develop and conduct appropriate experimentation and interpret data
- Ability to acquire and apply new knowledge using appropriate learning strategies
