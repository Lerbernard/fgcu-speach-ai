# Software Engineering B.S. — Degree Map

## Program Overview
The Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at FGCU is ABET-accredited. Software engineers use a systematic approach to the software life cycle including development, production, operation, and maintenance of large-scale software projects. Total credits required: 120.

Average salary for FGCU software engineering alumni: $76,144.
74% of graduates stay in Florida.

## Graduation Requirements
- Complete a minimum of 120 credits
- At least 42 upper division credits (3000-4999 level)
- Cumulative GPA of 2.0 at FGCU
- Minimum grade of C in each required course
- Satisfy Service-Learning, Civic Literacy, and Sustainability requirements
- CEN 4934 and CEN 4935 must be taken at FGCU

## Combined B.S. / M.S. Program

Qualified students can complete both the B.S. in Software Engineering and the M.S. in Computer Science in approximately 5 years.

**Admission Requirements:**
- Completed 75 credits in the B.S. in Software Engineering
- Major GPA of at least 3.4
- Overall GPA of at least 3.2

**Program Details:**
- B.S. is 120 credits, M.S. in Computer Science is 30 credits
- Up to 9 graduate credits double-counted for both degrees
- Total combined program: minimum 141 credit hours
- Minimum grade of B- required for each double-counted graduate course
- Graduate courses substitute for restricted elective courses in the bachelor's degree
