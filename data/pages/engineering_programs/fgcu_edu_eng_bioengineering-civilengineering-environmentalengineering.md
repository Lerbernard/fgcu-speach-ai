# Bioengineering, Civil Engineering & Environmental Engineering — Department Overview

Environmental and Civil Engineers design and construct the infrastructure of our society and are responsible for creating sustainable solutions to tomorrow's challenges while protecting the health of the public and the environment.

Both the Environmental Engineering and Civil Engineering programs have earned ABET accreditation. Students graduating from ABET-accredited programs are immediately able to sit for the Fundamentals of Engineering exam, one of the first steps in achieving professional licensure.

## Programs Offered
- Bioengineering (B.S.)
- Civil Engineering (B.S.C.E.)
- Environmental Engineering (B.S.Env.E.)

## Minors Offered
- Bioengineering Minor
- Environmental Engineering Minor
