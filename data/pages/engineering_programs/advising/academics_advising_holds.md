# Understanding University Holds

Students should habitually check their holds through GULFLINE by selecting "Student & Financial Aid" then "Student Records." This practice is essential to avoid any restrictions on registering for classes or making changes to the class schedule.

It is critical to take the steps that are necessary to remove all holds as soon as possible. Removing all holds allows processing time for departments, schools and agencies to send required information. Most holds restrict students from progressing with registration.

## Holds That Prevent Course Registration

AV — Advising Hold: Students who are admitted through the Student Success Pathways 1 and the Student Success Pathways 2 will have an advising hold. To make schedule changes, students will need to consult their Academic Advisor.

DH — Dean of Students Hold: Related to the conduct review process.

IM — Immunization Hold: See Immunization Requirements.

SC — Conduct Hold: Related to the conduct review process.

SU — Suspension Hold: See FGCU's Academic Standing Policy.

## Holds That Prevent Second-Semester Registration

CT — Final College Transcript Hold: Official transcripts must be sent to FGCU Undergraduate Admissions, Florida Gulf Coast University, Ft. Myers, FL 33965-6565. Faxed, copied, unsealed envelope submissions from former schools, or transcripts sent from a student's home are not official.
