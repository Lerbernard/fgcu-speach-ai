# U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering Advising

## We're Here to Help You Succeed
At the U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering Advising Office, your success is our mission. Our advising team is here to support you from your first semester through graduation. We'll help you plan your academic journey, understand university policies, make informed decisions, and connect with the right campus resources. Meeting with us early and often is the best way to stay on track and make the most of your FGCU experience.

## Advising Appointments

30-Minute Appointment, scheduled in advance, in-person or virtual. For: Academic Planning, Change Major/Minor, Transfer Advising.

Drop-In Advising (Monday–Friday), first come first served, in-person or virtual. For: Course Scheduling Issues, Registration Errors, Account Holds. Drop-In Advising will return August 12, 2026.

Email Advising for quick questions and common advising issues for students and non-students (General Questions, Permit Requests). Responses typically within 48 hours, excluding weekends and holidays. Email: WCEAdvising@fgcu.edu.

If you're ever unsure who your advisor is, just check the top section of your Degree Works audit—their name will be listed there.

**Are you a first-year student?**
If you're starting your first semester after high school, you'll begin with the Exploratory Advising team—not the WCE Advising Office. You'll stay with Exploratory Advising until you meet all of the following:
- Earn 24 college credits
- Complete MAC 1105 with a grade of C or better
- Maintain a 2.0 GPA

**Not a WCE student?**
If you're in a different college or school, please contact the advising center for your specific college or school.

## Destination FGCU Advising
If you're currently earning your AA degree at Florida SouthWestern State College and plan to transfer to FGCU, you're encouraged to explore the Destination FGCU program. This program is designed to support your transfer journey and help you prepare for success at FGCU.

Once you've completed 30 credit hours at FSW, you'll be eligible to meet with U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering Advisors. These are 30-minute virtual advising appointments, held on the first Friday of each month from 1:00–3:00 PM. To request an appointment with WCE Advising, complete the online request form.
