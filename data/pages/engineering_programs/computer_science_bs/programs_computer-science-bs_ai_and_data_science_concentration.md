# Computer Science B.S. — AI and Data Science Concentration

## What makes this concentration unique (also called the Ai And Data Science track, Ai And Data Science option, Ai And Data Science emphasis)
The courses that set the Ai And Data Science concentration apart from other Computer Science concentrations are: CAI 4002, CAI 4105, IDC 3140, STA 3038.
