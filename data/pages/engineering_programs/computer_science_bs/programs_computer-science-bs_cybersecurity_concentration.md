# Computer Science B.S. — Cybersecurity Concentration

## What makes this concentration unique (also called the Cybersecurity track, Cybersecurity option, Cybersecurity emphasis)
The courses that set the Cybersecurity concentration apart from other Computer Science concentrations are: CIS 3213, CIS 4224, CIS 4368, CIS 4622.
