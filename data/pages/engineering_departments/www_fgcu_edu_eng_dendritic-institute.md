About the Dendritic Institute 
Just as dendrites in the nervous system form interconnected networks facilitating the transmission of signals between neurons, Dendritic fosters connectivity among researchers, labs, and specialized tools to exchange ideas, knowledge, and solutions seamlessly. This name reflects the institute’s commitment to agility, adaptability, and innovation, mirroring the dynamic nature of dendritic structures in responding to stimuli.
