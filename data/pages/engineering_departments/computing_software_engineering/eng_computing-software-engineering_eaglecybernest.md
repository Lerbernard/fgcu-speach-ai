# EagleCyberNest — Cybersecurity Lab

The EagleCyberNest is a cybersecurity research and learning space at FGCU providing students with hands-on experience in network security, IoT security, and cutting-edge cybersecurity technologies.

## Ongoing Projects
- GenCyber Summer Camp — cybersecurity education for middle and high school teachers in Southwest Florida
- Cyber Clinic — hands-on training preparing students to address cyber threats in healthcare, energy, water infrastructure, and small businesses; collaborates with Shady Rest Institute, the Water School, and local governments
- National Cyber League Competition — FGCU began competing in Spring 2025; tests skills in cryptography, network traffic analysis, reverse engineering, web security, and forensics

## Student Opportunities
- Cybersecurity coursework in network security, IoT security, and wireless security
- Research projects on emerging cybersecurity threats and defense mechanisms
- Cybersecurity competitions including ethical hacking and security analysis
- Capstone and independent research projects

## Active Research Projects
- DroneCOCoNet — drone coordination system for disaster response, smart farming, and traffic management using machine learning and edge computing
- GANs in IoT Security — using Generative Adversarial Networks to improve intrusion detection systems for IoT networks
- Smart Home Security for Elderly — federated deep neural networks to protect smart home IoT devices used by seniors
- Edge Video Analytics — deep reinforcement learning for real-time video processing in smart cities and autonomous systems
- NetPrompt — LLM-driven network policy automation for Software-Defined Networking

## Lab Equipment
- High-performance desktops on campus DMZ network
- Enterprise-grade network switches, routers, and wireless devices
- IoT devices, smart home systems, and industrial sensors
- Embedded computing platforms for AI-driven security
- RF signal analysis and software-defined radio tools
- Drones and UAV systems for cybersecurity research
- Resources for ethical hacking, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessment
