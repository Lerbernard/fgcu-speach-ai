# DENDRITIC Institute — AI and Data Science

The Dendritic Institute is FGCU's Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Institute. It serves as a hub for innovation, research, and education in AI/DS, leveraging FGCU's resources and commitment to community engagement to pioneer groundbreaking solutions while preparing the next generation of AI and Data Science professionals.

The name "Dendritic" reflects the institute's commitment to connectivity — just as dendrites in the nervous system form networks transmitting signals between neurons, Dendritic fosters connectivity among researchers, labs, and specialized tools to exchange ideas and solutions.

## Branches
- FGCU AI Academy — educational programs and events for students, faculty, and professionals in AI/DS
- AI Excellence Hub — practical implementation and integration of AI technologies into academic, operational, and community systems
- AI Research Builder — supports development of basic and applied research in AI/DS by fostering collaboration and providing resources

## Mission
To advance education, research, development, and innovation in AI/DS, leading to professional development and the creation of knowledge, solutions, and technologies for the betterment of society.

## Goals
- Contribute to AI/DS advancement
- Establish prominence in Southwest Florida
- Forge collaborations with industry and government
- Advance education in AI and Data Science
- Complement and strengthen existing academic programs
