# Computer Science Minor — Requirements

## Admission Requirements
- Meet with a college advisor to declare the minor and review course requirements

## Additional Graduation Requirements
- Grade of C or higher required in all minor courses
- Minimum 12 credit hours of the minor completed at FGCU
- Minimum 9 credits must be upper division (3000-4999 level)
- Note the minor on the Application for Graduation

## Required Courses (18 total credits)
Complete all of the following:
- COP 1500 Intro to Computer Science (3 credits)
- COP 2006 Programming I (3 credits)
- COP 3003 Programming II (3 credits)
- COP 3530 Data Structures and Algorithms (3 credits)
- MAD 3107 Discrete Mathematics (3 credits)

Complete one from the following:
- CDA 3104 Computer Organization and Assembly Language Programming (3 credits)
- CDA 3200 Digital Systems and Architecture (3 credits)

**Total Credits Required: 18**
