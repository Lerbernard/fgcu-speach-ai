# Computer Science M.S. — Graduate Program

## Program Overview
The Master of Science in Computer Science at FGCU is a 30-credit program equipping students for careers in technology, finance, healthcare, and more. Tuition is less than $12,000 total.

## Program Details
- 30 credit-hour degree program
- Full or part-time options available
- Some classes offered remotely
- Tuition under $12,000
- Financial aid and tuition support available
- Thesis and non-thesis tracks available

## Concentrations
- Data Science — AI, machine learning, data analytics
- Cybersecurity — network security, secure systems
- No Concentration

## Admissions Requirements
- Minimum overall GPA of 3.2
- Application period: open to new applicants
- Scheduled meeting with the program coordinator

## Combined B.S./M.S. Program
Fast-track through the combined B.S. to M.S. program. Complete up to 9 graduate credits as an undergraduate, saving time and money. Eligible majors: Computer Science, Software Engineering.

Combined program admission requirements:
- Minimum overall GPA of 3.2
- Application period: sophomore to junior year
- Scheduled meeting with the program coordinator

## Graduate Assistantships
A limited number of graduate assistantships are available for full-time students (9+ credits/semester). Assistants work 20 hours per week teaching and/or conducting directed research. To be considered, indicate interest in your Statement of Objectives when applying.
