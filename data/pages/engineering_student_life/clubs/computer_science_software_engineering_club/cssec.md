# Computer Science & Software Engineering Club
## About
The Computer Science & Software Engineering Club (CSSEC) is an inclusive, vibrant student organization that is open to all majors—whether you’re a coding newbie or a tech enthusiast, you’ll feel right at home here. We’re passionate about building a supportive community around technology, innovation, and career development. Plus, there’s dinner at every meeting!
Each year, we host **EagleHacks** , an annual hackathon sponsored by local companies, where students team up to tackle real-world challenges, win exciting prizes, and network with industry professionals.
Our regular meetings cover a variety of topics and events, including:
  * **Coding Workshops** : Learn new programming languages, enhance your coding skills, and explore tech tools in hands-on workshops that cater to all skill levels.
  * **Social Events** : Connect with fellow members in a laid-back environment at our **Game Nights** or other fun events, where you can win prizes and enjoy free food.

Whether you’re here to grow your technical skills, build your professional network, or just have fun with like-minded people, CSSEC has something for you! Join us at our next meeting—grab a bite, expand your tech knowledge, and get involved in our ever-growing community!
