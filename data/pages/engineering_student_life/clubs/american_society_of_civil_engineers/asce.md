# American Society of Civil Engineers
## About
**Join ASCE:** Ignite your engineering journey with the most prominent club on campus. Our mission is to empower a community of innovative engineers who thrive on challenges, question everything, and seek out answers while building skills like teamwork, integrity, and professionalism. Through hands-on projects and thrilling competitions, we go beyond textbooks, shaping the future of engineering. Network with professionals, access workshops, and bridge the gap between education and application. As we engineer sustainable solutions and uplift communities, our impact is lasting. Partnered with the Southwest Florida Branch, we offer opportunities to attend presentations by professional engineers and community leaders, enriching your experience. Join us in engineering excellence, transforming aspirations into achievements. Let's Go!
