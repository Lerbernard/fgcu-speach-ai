# IEEE Student Branch at FGCU
## About
**What We Do:** IEEE works to form the engineers of tomorrow by co-locating and hosting dynamic tech events, professional networking opportunities, technical workshops, and hands-on projects. 
**Benefits of IEEE Student Membership:**
  * Access to the IEEE Xplore Digital Library (5M+ technical documents)
  * Networking with 420,000+ members worldwide
  * Career development resources, resume tools, and job boards
  * Eligibility for IEEE scholarships, awards, and competitions
