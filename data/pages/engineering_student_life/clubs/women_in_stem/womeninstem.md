# Women in STEM
## About
Women in STEM Club is a community dedicated to uplift and prepare women for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our goal is to create a welcoming space where members feel confident, capable, and encouraged to thrive in fields that have not always been women-led.
Through hands-on workshops, speaker events, mentorship opportunities, and collaborative projects, we focus on building both technical skills and personal confidence. We highlight the achievements of women in STEM, share real-world advice on navigating academic and professional spaces, and provide tools to help members advocate for themselves and their ideas.
This club is about more than just academics; it’s about leadership, resilience, and belonging. Whether you’re just beginning to explore STEM or are already deeply involved, you’ll find a community that celebrates curiosity, ambition, and growth. Together, we aim to challenge stereotypes, support one another’s goals, and prepare women to not only succeed in STEM fields, but to lead, innovate, and make lasting change. All are welcome to join us as we learn, connect, and build confidence!
