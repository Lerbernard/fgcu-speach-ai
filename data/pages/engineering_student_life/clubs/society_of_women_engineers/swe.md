# Society of Women Engineers
## About
The Society of Women Engineers (SWE) at Florida Gulf Coast University is a vibrant community dedicated to empowering women to achieve their full potential as engineers, innovators, and leaders. We aim to redefine the image of engineering by promoting diversity, inclusion, and collaboration while inspiring the next generation of changemakers. SWE at FGCU provides a supportive and welcoming environment for all majors through professional development, mentorship, outreach, and fun events that build confidence and connection. Join us as we work together to shape a brighter, more inclusive future in engineering and technology! We can’t wait to SWE you at our events !!💜⚙️
