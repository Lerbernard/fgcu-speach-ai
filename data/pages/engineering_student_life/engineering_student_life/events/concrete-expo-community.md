Concrete Exposure Day
Each year FGCU, the Florida Department of Transportation, and the Florida Concrete & Products Association join together to showcase the latest concrete industry practices. Professionals may receive up professional development hours for attending all sessions. Certificates will be provided at the conclusion of the last session. This year's Concrete Expo will be held on **October 27, 2026**.
