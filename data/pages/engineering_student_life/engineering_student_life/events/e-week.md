**Engineers Week (E-Week)**

Engineers Week is part of a national, annual celebration of engineering. Throughout the week, students can attend a series of events put on by student organizations and the college. E-Week allows students to explore real-world applications of engineering, connect with guest speakers and industry professionals, and learn about different fields and career opportunities. E-Week events can include networking sessions, student organization activities, and fun traditions like the annual Students vs. Faculty Tug-of-War.
