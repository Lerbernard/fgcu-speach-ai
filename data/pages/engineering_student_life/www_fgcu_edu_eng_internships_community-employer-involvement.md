### Community Events
  * Concrete Expo

Concrete Exposure Day
Each year FGCU, the Florida Department of Transportation, and the Florida Concrete & Products Association join together to showcase the latest concrete industry practices. Professionals may receive up professional development hours for attending all sessions. Certificates will be provided at the conclusion of the last session. This year's Concrete Expo will be held on **October 27, 2026**.
  * Collier County School Visits
Toggle More Info
Each year over 3,200 Collier County 7th graders are brought out by the Collier County School District to FGCU for a campus tour and to engage in various engineering activities. The dates are determined by the Collier County School District in collaboration with the University. 
  * Sunchase Solar Go-Kart Challenge
Toggle More Info
In 2013 Dr. Joe Simmons, the first Backe Chair for Renewable Energy at FGCU, had a vision to develop area high school student interest in engineering and renewable energy technologies through a solar go-kart competition held on campus. Since that first year the FGCU Faculty, Students, and Staff have worked hard to host and promote this event, and we hope to continue to work with each of you to continue to improve SunChase every year!
