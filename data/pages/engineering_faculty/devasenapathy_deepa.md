Department of Computing & Software Engineering

Dr. Deepa Devasenapathy is a dedicated educator and scholar with over two decades of experience in computer science and engineering education. She currently serves as a faculty member in the Department of Computing & Software Engineering at Florida Gulf Coast University, where she teaches courses including Introduction to Computer Science, Programming I and II, Software Testing, and Digital Systems and Architecture. Her research interests span machine learning, data science education, human-centered AI, and innovative pedagogical practices. Dr. Devasenapathy has published multiple peer-reviewed journal articles, conference papers, patents, and a book chapter, and she actively contributes to the academic community as a reviewer and committee member. She is committed to fostering student success, integrating emerging technologies into the curriculum, and advancing AI literacy within STEM education.
