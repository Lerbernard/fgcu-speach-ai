## Bao, Jieyi
**Assistant Professor, Construction Management**
Department of Construction Management
239-590-7685
jbao@fgcu.edu
Dr. Jieyi Bao is an Assistant Professor in the Stock Development Department of Construction Management at Florida Gulf Coast University. She earned her Ph.D. from Purdue University, where her research focused on transportation infrastructure safety, smart construction technologies, and performance evaluation of pavement systems. She teaches courses in construction finance, materials, equipment, and building systems. Her teaching emphasizes practical application, critical thinking, and technology integration to prepare students for professional success in the construction industry.

**Education**

    * M.Econ. in Investment Project Management Institute, Dongbei University of Finance and Economics
    * B.S. in School of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, Jilin University of Finance and Economics
