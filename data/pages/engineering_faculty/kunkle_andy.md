## Kunkle, Andy
**Construction Management, Adjunct Faculty**
Department of Construction Management
askunkle@fgcu.edu
Andy Kunkle is a technology leader with over 25 years of experience in IT, data analytics, and software development across industries including construction, healthcare, and biotechnology. At FGCU, he teaches AI in Construction, a course designed to equip future construction professionals and industry leaders with the tools to turn data into action on the job site and in the boardroom.  
Andy has built enterprise-scale analytics platforms, led digital transformation initiatives using Microsoft Power Platform and Power BI, and worked with national construction firms to modernize decision-making through data. He is passionate about helping students understand how data and AI are reshaping the built environment.
