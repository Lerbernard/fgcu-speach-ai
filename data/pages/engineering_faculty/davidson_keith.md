Keith Davidson joined Florida Gulf Coast University in January 2021 as the Assistant Director for Career Advising in the office of Career Development Services. In April 2023, he was promoted to the role of Director of Academic Advising for the U.A. Whitaker College of Engineering. He has spent his entire career working for regional-comprehensive universities in a variety of different advising and student success roles.
Keith has been an active member of NACADA: The Global Community for Academic Advising since 2016 and has assisted in planning conferences for the organization at the Maryland State and Region II level. He is also a Gallup-Certified Strengths Coach who is passionate about helping others discover the value of their innate talents.
