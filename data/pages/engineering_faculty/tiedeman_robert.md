## Tiedeman, Robert
**Adjunct, Construction Management**
Department of Construction Management
rtiedeman@fgcu.edu
Twenty years of professional experience in construction project management in public and private sectors. Seven years facilities management experience for over 600 government facilities ranging in size from 300 SF to 1,000,000 SF. Expert knowledge of project management with experience in all phases of project life cycle including the facilitation of project definition, bid solicitation, contract negotiation, to award and execution. Experienced in managing all key project activities to ensure optimum scope/configuration control, cost/schedule compliance, and quality assurance/control of project designs, specifications, contracting strategies, and contractor performance leading to claims avoidance/claims mitigation.
