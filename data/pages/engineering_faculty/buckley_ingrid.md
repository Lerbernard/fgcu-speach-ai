Dr. Ingrid Buckley is an Assistant Professor in the Software Engineering Department at Florida Gulf Coast University. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Florida Atlantic University. Dr. Buckley’s research interests include software engineering education, fault-tolerant system design, and cybersecurity. Dr. Buckley is a member of the Upsilon Pi Epsilon, Golden Key International Honor Society, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and the American Society for Engineering Education (ASEE).
