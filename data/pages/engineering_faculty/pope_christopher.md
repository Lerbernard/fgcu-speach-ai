## Pope, Christopher
**Construction Management, Adjunct Faculty**
Department of Construction Management
clpope@fgcu.edu
Christopher Pope has been an adjunct faculty in Construction Management since Spring 2022. He is recognized by the Florida Bar as a Board Certified Specialist in three practice areas, Condominium and Planned Development Law; Real Estate Law; and Construction Law (Board certification is the highest level of recognition by the Florida Bar for the competency and experience of a lawyer practicing in a specialty area, as less than 7% of Florida lawyers are board certified). Mr. Pope primarily focuses his practice in the areas of condominium and homeowners’ association law; construction law; and real estate.
