# Undergraduate Admissions

## Start Your Journey to Success
At FGCU you'll get comprehensive instruction and hands-on learning opportunities that empower you to excel and shape your life's journey as a leader, innovator and engaged citizen.

## Applicant Types
First-Time in College / Freshman, Transfer / Second Bachelor's Degree, Dual Enrollment / Accelerated College Experience Program, Non-Traditional (including Veterans), International, Readmit / Return to the Nest, and Non-Degree.

## Paying for College
FGCU is committed to helping you find a path to an affordable education through scholarships, grants, waivers and other resources.
- How to Get Started: Financial aid is an important step. Get details, eligibility information and key dates.
- How to Apply for Aid: Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic achievement, financial need, and/or other eligibility requirements.
- Work Study Opportunities: Explore work study opportunities and eligibility requirements.
- Cost of Attendance: Learn about tuition, fees, books and overall cost of attendance.

## FGCU Snapshot

- Students attending FGCU: 15,892 (87% are undergraduate)
