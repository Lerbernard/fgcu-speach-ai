# Graduate Admissions

## Flexible Graduate and Professional Programs
FGCU offers 28 master's degrees, 7 doctoral degrees and 19 certificate programs that prepare you for today's most innovative fields.

**Flexible Learning Options**
FGCU offers online, hybrid, and in-person pathways that support working professionals and diverse student needs. Verify the method of study for your graduate program of interest by visiting the Graduate Programs Directory.

**Personalized Support**
From your first inquiry to enrollment and beyond, FGCU's dedicated faculty and staff provide personalized guidance to help navigate your graduate journey.

**Affordable, High-Value Education in SW Florida**
FGCU combines competitive tuition rates with the advantages of Southwest Florida's rapidly growing economy, vibrant communities, and unique environmental landscapes.
