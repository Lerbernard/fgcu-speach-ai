# Admissions & Aid at FGCU

## Your Future Starts Here at FGCU
FGCU gives you access to extraordinary academics, caring faculty and endless opportunities. Whether you are a freshman or a transfer student, seeking graduate studies or looking for the flexibility of online classes, we are dedicated to supporting personal growth while upholding academic excellence.

Main areas: Undergraduate Admissions, Graduate Admissions, Financial Aid & Scholarships, and Visit Campus.

## FGCU by the Numbers
- 64% graduate with zero debt
- No tuition increase in 11 years
- $134M in financial aid & scholarship
- $39M in scholarships & waivers

FGCU's 66 undergraduate, 28 graduate and 7 doctoral programs provide opportunities and hands-on experiences. The 22:1 student-to-faculty ratio means an education with a personal touch.
