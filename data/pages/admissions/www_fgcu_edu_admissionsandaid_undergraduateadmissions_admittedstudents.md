# After You've Been Admitted

Your admissions package contains helpful information about how to prepare for the start of classes.

**Say Yes to the Nest Admitted Student Day** helps you connect to FGCU, see what services and resources are available, and meet faculty and staff in your desired major.

## Accepting Your Admission Decision

**Claim your Eagle Access account**
The next step in your enrollment process is to claim your Eagle Access account, which gives you access to the FGCU email system, EagleMail. University offices and staff/faculty send important information to this address and may not respond to communication from personal email accounts. It is your responsibility to routinely monitor and manage your EagleMail. Claiming the account also enables access to other FGCU systems such as Gulfline.
To claim your account: go to www.fgcu.edu/myaccount, then enter your First Name, Last Name, and UIN exactly as they appear on your admittance email. Complete all steps before exiting; otherwise contact the University Help Desk at 239-590-1188 to reset account claiming.(Email: helpdesk@fgcu.edu)

**Provide your Decision Reply and Submit your Admission Deposit**
To accept your offer of admission, log into your Future Eagle Account and answer your Decision Reply question (on the Home tab, right-hand column under "Forms"). After accepting, submit your $200 non-refundable deposit via the "Payment Due" section. Once you submit your deposit, a seat is held for you. The deposit deadline for Fall is May 1st; deposits may be accepted after the deadline until the term is full. The admission deposit is applicable to the term for which the student has been admitted and is non-refundable. It will be forfeited if the student does not enroll in the deposited term. If a request to change terms is made after classes for the original term have begun, a deposit for the new term must be paid.

**Register for Orientation**
All new students must attend Eagle View Orientation (EVO). Directions for Summer and Fall sessions are sent starting in February; for Spring, emails begin in October. Register early for the best course selection. Orientation is held in late fall for the Spring term and throughout the summer for the Summer and Fall terms.

**Apply for Housing**
If you plan to live on campus, apply early. There are three housing options: apartment-style living in North Lake or West Lake Villages, or suite-style living in South Village, which features the First Year Residential Experience.

**Establish your Residency**
All students must establish residency as a Florida Resident or non-Florida resident for tuition purposes. New students are considered non-Florida residents until proper documentation is received. Completing residency is essential for correct tuition billing, financial aid awarding, and merit scholarship eligibility. Complete the Residency Form via the Home screen of your Future Eagle account, under "Forms." If you indicate you are a Florida Resident, you will need to provide supporting documentation.

**Apply for Financial Aid/Scholarships**
March 1st is the FGCU priority deadline for the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and the FGCU Foundation Scholarships. The federal school code is 032553. The Foundation Scholarships application closes on March 1st. Apply annually by the priority deadline.

**Visit Campus**
You are invited to attend a visit day or take a campus tour.

**Immunization Requirements**
All students are required to present documented vaccine information.
